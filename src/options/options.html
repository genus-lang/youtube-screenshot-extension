How to Use

Get the most out of your YouTube Screenshot extension.

Manual Capture & OCR

  • Take a Screenshot: Click the 📸 icon on the YouTube player bar (or use the floating button, or extension popup). The extension captures the slide at the video's highest resolution.
  • Automatic OCR: Your screenshot is automatically scanned offline to extract text (OCR). You can edit this text right away to save important points to your notes.
  • Pro Tip: Keep your YouTube video quality as high as possible (e.g., 1080p) for the best text extraction results.
AI Powered

Smart Auto-Capture

  • Hands-Free Mode: Turn on "Auto Capture" in the extension popup. The extension will watch the video and automatically take a screenshot when it detects a new presentation slide.
  • Smart Presenter Detection: If a teacher is standing in front of the presentation slide, the extension's built-in AI detects them and waits! It will actively hold off until the teacher steps away, and automatically capture the clean, unblocked slide once they move.
  • Keep Active: For Auto-Capture and AI face detection to work flawlessly, try to keep the YouTube tab active or in its own window.

Exporting Notes

  • PDF Export: Download all your screenshots and notes cleanly formatted into a single PDF document.
  • OneNote Export: Push your captured frames and notes directly into a Microsoft OneNote notebook.
  • Offline Security: Your screenshots and OCR processing are handled entirely locally on your device for absolute privacy.

About
