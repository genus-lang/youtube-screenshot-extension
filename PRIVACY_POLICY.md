# Privacy Policy for YouTube Screenshot Notes

**Effective Date:** April 4, 2026

Thank you for using **YouTube Screenshot Notes** (the "Extension"). This Privacy Policy outlines what information the Extension collects, why it is collected, and how it is used.

Privacy Policy for YouTube Screenshot Notes

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

The extension only accesses YouTube video content locally in the user's browser to capture screenshots when the user interacts with the extension.

No data is transmitted to any external server.

All functionality runs locally on the user's device.
